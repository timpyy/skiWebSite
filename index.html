Jay Peak, VT

  • February 1-3, 2019
  • $245
  • Registration Status: NOT OPEN YET

Situated right on the border with Canada, Jay Peak is one of our most popular trips of the year so be sure to sign up fast! The mountain consistently receives more snowfall than any other resort in the Northeast, so satisfaction is always guaranteed! For this weekend trip, we will be staying at Slopeside Condominiums, where you can easily ski in and out right from your backdoor!

Lake Placid, NY

  • February 22-24, 2019
  • $259
  • Registration Status: NOT OPEN YET

Home to the 1980 Winter Olympics, Whiteface Mountain at Lake Placid has the greatest vertical of any lift-serviced mountain in the Northeast! This weekend trip will take place in late February, and we will be staying at the Hampton Inn and Suites in the heart of the village. The mountain is a short bus ride away from the resort, and there is plenty of nightlife right at your doorstep.

Breckenridge, CO

  • March 16-23, 2019
  • ~$1719 (subject to decrease)
  • Registration Status: OPEN
  • More Info

This week-long trip takes place over spring break in the majestic Colorado Rockies! We will be staying at River Mountain Lodge, which is a short hop away from the mountain. An absolute must go! Prices for this trip include plane tickets, transportation from the airport to the resort, lodging, and lift passes for the duration of the trip.
